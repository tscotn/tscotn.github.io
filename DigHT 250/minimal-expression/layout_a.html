ANGEL OLSEN

As one of my favorite artists to listen to this year, grayscale colors echo the mood but not the richness of Olsen's sound, as well as the scheme of her critically acclaimed album "All Mirrors".

The sharp corners of the font "Poiret One" line up well with the wryness and drama of her voice.

BIG THIEF

"Masterpiece", "Cattails", and "Shark Smile" are some real good tracks from this Brooklyn-based quartet. Earthy, human tones characterize their album art and their music.

Font: Vollkorn. The bulky serifs of this font echo the polished folkiness of this band.

Goth Babe

Goth Babe is the one man project of Griff Washburn, who spends his time making dreamy tracks in his RV between playing with his dog and surfing.

Font: Caveat Brush is as fuzzy and homemade as the synths and drums on "Her Vacation" and "Weekend Friend".
